### 临时
```
var priString = 'I am a string';
console.log(typeof priString);            //String
console.log( priString instanceof String);  //false

var objString = new String('I am a string');
console.log(typeof objString);            //object
console.log( objString instanceof String);  //true
```
*第一段相当于执行了toString()方法返回的结果*
